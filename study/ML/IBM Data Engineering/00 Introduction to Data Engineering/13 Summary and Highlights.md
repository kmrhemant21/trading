## Entry Paths into Data Engineering

- **Academic route:** Computer Science or engineering degree for entry-level positions
- **Professional certifications:** Online specializations from platforms like Coursera, edX, and Udacity
- **Upskilling from related roles:**
    - IT Support Specialists
    - Software Testers
    - Programmers
    - Data professionals (Statisticians, Data Analysts, BI Analysts)
