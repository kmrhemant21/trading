# Discussion: Explainable AI

## Discussion prompt 
Reflect on your experience with AI grading in your last assignment. Consider how you felt about having your work evaluated by an AI system and whether you found the process fair and accurate, providing reasons for your perspective. Reflect on the ethical aspects of AI grading by evaluating whether it met standards such as transparency, accountability, and consistency. Suggest any specific improvements that could make AI grading systems more effective, trustworthy, and equitable. Additionally, compare AI grading to human grading, discussing whether you believe AI can match or surpass human judgment, particularly for assessing open-ended responses, and explain why. Use these guiding questions to shape your response, but feel free to explore other aspects of your experience. This reflection is an opportunity to critically evaluate the ethical and practical dimensions of AI in education and share your thoughtful and honest insights on its role in assessment.

## Instructions
1. **Be specific**: reflect on your experience with AI grading your last assignment. Clearly describe your thoughts on key aspects, such as how fair and accurate you found the process, how it felt to have your work evaluated by an AI system, and any challenges you noticed.

2. **Explain your perspective**: discuss whether the AI grading process was ethical. Evaluate whether it met important principles such as transparency, accountability, and consistency in its evaluation. Provide examples or reasoning to support your perspective.

3. **Suggest improvements**: offer specific recommendations for enhancing the effectiveness, trustworthiness, and fairness of AI grading systems. Consider technological, ethical, or practical changes that could address any limitations you observed.

4. **Reflect on comparison with human grading**: share your views on how AI grading compares to human grading. Consider whether AI can match or surpass human judgment for open-ended responses, explaining your reasoning and any trade-offs involved.

This reflection will help you and your peers critically analyze the role of AI in assessment, fostering a deeper understanding of its ethical, practical, and educational dimensions.
